# Review Mission Design - v2.4.0

## Structure Overview

### Regular Reviews (10-15 objectives each)
- **Review 1** - After Mission 5 (Basics: ls, pwd, cd, cat, head, tail, cp, mv, rm, touch, mkdir)
- **Review 2** - After Mission 10 (Intermediate: grep variants, find, head/tail advanced, pipes, redirection)
- **Review 3** - After Mission 15 (Advanced: wildcards, complex paths, combining skills)

### Advanced Review (20+ objectives)
- **Final Review** - After Mission 15 (Comprehensive: Everything from missions 1-15)

## Learning Objectives

### Review 1: Foundations (Missions 1-5)
**Commands to test:**
- ls, pwd, cd (navigation)
- cat, head, tail (file viewing)
- touch, mkdir (file/dir creation)
- cp, mv, rm (file operations)

**Objectives (12 total):**
Mix of navigation, file viewing with different commands, creating/copying/moving/deleting files

### Review 2: Search & Analysis (Missions 6-10)
**Commands to test:**
- grep (basic and flags: -i, -n, -c, -v)
- find (with -name and -type)
- head/tail with -n flags
- Pipes (|)
- wc (-l, -w)
- Redirection (>, >>)

**Objectives (15 total):**
Mix of searching, filtering, piping, counting, and saving output

### Review 3: Advanced Operations (Missions 11-15)
**Commands to test:**
- Output redirection (>, >>)
- Wildcards (*)
- Complex navigation (../.., absolute paths)
- Multi-command pipelines
- Complex grep patterns
- Batch operations

**Objectives (15 total):**
Real-world scenarios combining multiple skills

### Advanced Review: Complete Mastery
**Commands to test:**
Everything from missions 1-15

**Objectives (20-25 total):**
Comprehensive real-world scenarios testing full command mastery

## Reward Structure
- Regular Reviews: 500 XP each (same as complex missions)
- Advanced Review: 1500 XP (major milestone)
